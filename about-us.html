About Us!

Sometimes, at a certain point of life, we stuck at a moment, a phase that messes with our mind. It affects our lifestyle, our way of living, our productivity, our health and most importantly, "Our Happiness". We all need a shoulder to cry on, we all need someone to vent out the things that are killing us from inside. We want to be heard, but only some of the lucky people here got someone who actually cares about them, who are really there when you need them. And sometimes explaining things that you are feeling is just next to impossible .Sometimes, you want to be left alone and simultaneously want to be sure that someone is standing with you, someone who is there to hold your hand. We all crave for support, love, care and happiness, but that moment hits so hard that we can't get back up on our own.
If you feel the same way, you should talk to your close friends, family about what you are feeling. And we are here too to listen to you, to make you feel better and positive. This society laughs at someone's vulnerability. but we have to change that. We will give you the right advice to make a better "YOU". You have believe that you are Important! At the end, you will believe that, life is beautiful!
We want to assure you that our chats will be anonymous, all the things that you share with one of our empathist will stay between you and them ONLY. So you can just empty your heavy heart without any hesitation :)


If you have anything in your mind, don't mind in contacting us !
